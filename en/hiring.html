Sales Consultant

Support clients bridging Japan and the Philippines — from documentation and consultations
to helping grow our cross-border business.

Real Estate B2B & Individual Recruitment Southeast Asia

About the Role

Inspire Holdings is a consulting company connecting Japan and the Philippines — covering real estate investment, company formation, IT systems, and lifestyle relocation.

As a Sales Consultant, you will bridge Japanese clients and our Philippine team from first consultation through contract and aftercare. Experience in sales is valued, and interest in cross-cultural work is essential.

Key Responsibilities

  • Plan & Propose SolutionsUnderstand client needs and propose optimal plans for real estate, visas, company setup, and more.
  • Coordinate OperationsWork with Philippine staff, lawyers, and agents to keep projects on track.
  • Document SupportAssist with contracts, applications, and reports — including translation support where needed.
  • CRM & ReportingManage client data, progress updates, and internal meetings using CRM tools.

Requirements

  • Interest in the Philippine business environment and real estate market
  • English or Tagalog communication skills (conversational level or above)
  • 3+ years in sales, consulting, or related fields
  • Experience with CRM tools (Salesforce, HubSpot, etc.)
  • Ability to collaborate in a multicultural team
  • Willingness to travel to the Philippines occasionally (a few times per year)
