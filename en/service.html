BGC (BONIFACIO GLOBAL CITY) ADVANTAGES

Located in Taguig City in Metro Manila, BGC is the Philippines' premier business and lifestyle hub. World-class infrastructure, a concentration of highly skilled English-speaking professionals, and an exceptionally safe environment make it the ideal Asian base for foreign companies. Our office is based in BGC — enabling seamless support from your first site visit through contract signing.

  • World-class office buildings and co-working spaces throughout the district
  • Dense concentration of Japanese and multinational firms — ideal for rapid network building
  • International schools, Japanese-speaking medical clinics, and premium residences for expatriate families

PEZA (PHILIPPINE ECONOMIC ZONE AUTHORITY) INCENTIVES

PEZA-registered companies receive significant advantages in taxation, customs, and foreign ownership rules. We provide end-to-end support: requirements analysis, application filing, and ongoing post-registration compliance.

  • Income Tax Holiday (ITH): Corporate tax exemption for 4–8 years after registration
  • Preferential rate: 5% Special Income Tax (GIT) applicable after ITH expires
  • Duty & VAT exemption on imported capital equipment and raw materials
  • 100% foreign ownership: PEZA enterprises are exempt from foreign equity restrictions
  • Special Employment Visa: Accelerated processing for foreign executives and staff
