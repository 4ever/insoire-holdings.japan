— WHAT WE DO

Our Expertise

Comprehensive support for every stage of your international journey — from first inquiry to long-term success.

REAL ESTATE

Philippine Real Estate

Access to residential and investment properties in BGC, Makati, and Pasig. We handle everything from property selection and purchase to rental management and eventual resale — all in one place.

Yields of 6–8% p.a. available on select listings
BUSINESS

Business Formation

We advise on the right corporate structure — OPC, stock corporation, or joint venture — then manage SEC registration, BIR tax enrollment, and local permits end-to-end, backed by our lawyer and CPA network.

Full guidance on foreign ownership rules (60/40)
LIFESTYLE

Relocation Support

From neighborhood selection in Manila, Cebu, or Davao to introductions to Japanese-affiliated hospitals, international schools, and daily infrastructure. We also advise on SRRV and 9G visa options.

Site visit arrangement and on-ground accompaniment
CONSULTING

Market Intelligence

Objective analysis of foreign ownership regulations, tax structures, political risk, and local business practices. We support feasibility studies pre-entry and contingency planning for market exit scenarios.

Industry-specific risk reports available on request
EDUCATION

Education & Language

Leverage the Philippines' English-speaking environment for short or long-term language study. We assist with international school placement for families and business English programs for working professionals.

Direct referrals to Inspire Holdings-operated programs
HEALTH & TOURISM

Healthcare & Tourism

Facilitation and information services for St. Luke's, Makati Medical Center, and other leading hospitals. We also support medical tourism arrangements, health screenings, and long-stay medical insurance queries.

Bespoke arrangements for HNW individuals and corporates
