🏠 Real Estate Acquisition Support

Foreigners cannot own land in the Philippines, but condominium units can be acquired as long as foreign ownership in a building does not exceed 40%. We also advise on structures involving Philippine entities for other property types. All transactions are handled through our licensed local partner network.
We primarily cover Metro Manila (BGC, Makati, Pasig), Cebu, and Clark. We have particularly strong knowledge of BGC (Bonifacio Global City), the most active area for Japanese corporate and individual clients.
